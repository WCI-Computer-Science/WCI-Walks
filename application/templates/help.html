About this app

Log your distance, compete for the leaderboard, check out your friends' progress, see statistics, and more! This guide walks you through each component of the app.

Logging In

To login, you must have registered with the school first. After that, we need to manually input your email in the system, so it may be a little bit before you are able to login.

Once you've been registered, select "Log In" from the menu at the top right of the screen. You must use your WRDSB email.

Adding Walks

You have two options to add your walks. You can sync with Google Fit on you phone to do it automatically (recommended), or you can manually input them yourself.

Google Fit

To sync with Google Fit, you first need to download the Google Fit app on your phone, and sign in with your WRDSB account.
Allow Google Fit permission to access all health data, and allow it to track your activities. Google Fit will not be able to track your walk progress otherwise.

After you've done that, head to your Profile and click on "Connect to Google Fit". Now WCI Walks will automatically get distance from your Google Fit account.

However, in order to get distances to show up, you need to ensure they've shown up in the Google Fit app. After you've done walking, make sure you have a WiFi connection, and open the Google Fit app on your phone. Reload the app until the correct distance appears. This is important, since Google Fit doesn't always load distances from your phone right away.

Manual Inputting

This option isn't recommended, and should only be used when necessary. To manually input distances, simply head to your Profile, enter your distance in km into the form, and click submit.

Statistics

Check your progress with statistics! From your Profile, you can see how much you've walked and a chart that shows how far you've walked each day.

You also see which position on the leaderboard you're at, as well as how many likes you have (see Social Features!).

From the home page, you can also see how far everybody has walked as a whole.

Social Features

There's a couple social features built into this app too!

The top 30 people will be shown on the leaderboard. There's an all-time leaderboard for total walk distance, as well as a day leaderboard which ranks whoever walked the day before.

To see someone else's walk statistics, simply click on their name on the leaderboard. You can "like" them by clicking on the heart icon beside their name. Don't worry, likes are anonymous!

If you still have any questions, feel free to contact us!
