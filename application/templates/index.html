About this app

Welcome to WCI Walks, a web app that helps you track your walkathon distances. Log your distance each day, compete with others on the leaderboards, or check out your friends' progress! You can also check out your own walking statistics from your profile.
To get started, please login from the menu using your WRDSB account.
