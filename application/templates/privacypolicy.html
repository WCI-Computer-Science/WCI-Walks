Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy.

So much, in fact, that we barely store any information about you at all. For legal reasons, however, we have laid out this privacy policy.

This Privacy Policy ("this policy") applies to the data collection and usage for all users of the {{ app_name() }} website ("this website" or "this site"), which is managed by the WCI Computer Science Club ("we", "us", or "our"). PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY IN ITS ENTIRETY, as by using this site, you consent to this policy and our Terms of Service.

Information We Collect

No information about you is obtained unless you log in. That means if you contact us outside of the app, such as through email, we do not store any of that information in the app, nor do we associate it with your account unless you explicitly request us to.

We store information about you when you log in, submit distances on your profile, or perform any other action on this site linked to your account. The personal information we store about you is limited to your first and last name, your WRDSB email, and the distance you've traveled {% if ui_settings.enableStrava %}(whether that be distance you manually input, or distance taken from Strava){% endif %}. {% if ui_settings.enableStrava %}We will request Strava data from you, but won't access it unless you enable automatic distance tracking from your profile.{% endif %}

We reserve the right to share your name and your distance information, which is necessary in order to perform our leaderboard rankings.

Security and Accuracy

Your information is always kept safe and secure. We use an encrypted https connection, so your data is safe in transit. Additionally, your personal data is stored in a Heroku PostgreSQL database, which is only accessible to us. We have taken steps to ensure maximum security of your data, but no technology is infallible. In the off chance that a data breach occurs, we will ensure transparency, and will notify all users of this site with a notice and via email. We are not liable for any breaches of data, or any consequences thereof.

We want to help keep your information always as up to date as possible. If any changes occur to your personal information and you would like those changes to be reflected in this website, or if you would like to delete your account, please email us at wcicomputerscience@gmail.com.

Authentication and Cookies

We authenticate users with Google using the OAuth 2.0 protocol. This system is secure, and ensures that only you are able to get into your account.

This site uses cookies solely for authentication. That means we only use a cookie in order to keep you logged in, so that our site knows who you are. We do not have any advertisements, nor work with any other third parties apart from the ones laid out in Third Party Sites.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
